KYCC PLAYERS

MATCH RULES :

1. Match Format

  • Each match will be played with **10 overs per side**.
  • Bowling limit: Maximum **2 overs per bowler**.
  • Both teams must report **15 minutes before match start**.
  • Only **tennis/rubber ball** allowed. No hardball.

2. Powerplay Rules

  • First **2 overs** are Powerplay.
  • Only **2 fielders** allowed outside the 30-yard circle.
  • No more than **5 fielders** allowed on leg side at any time.

3. Bowling & No-Ball Rules

  • Front foot no-ball → **Free Hit** next ball.
  • Over-the-waist full toss → **No-ball** + free hit.
  • Ball pitching outside the pitch → **Dead Ball**.
  • Bowler must bowl within **60 seconds** per delivery (slow over penalty applies).

4. Batting Rules

  • Retired hurt allowed only with **umpire approval**.
  • Last man batting allowed **only if team agrees before match**.
  • Only proper cricket bat allowed. No broken/damaged bats.

5. Fielding Rules

  • No fielder allowed to stand in the **danger area** during delivery.
  • Overthrow runs are valid even if ball hits the fielder.
  • Boundary catch: Fielder must not touch the rope at the moment of catch.

6. LBW & Wide Rules

  • LBW is applicable in this tournament.
  • Wide: Ball going outside the line of the pitch is considered wide.
  • Leg-side wide applies for balls outside leg stump.

7. Super Over Rules

  • If scores tie → **Super Over** will decide the winner.
  • 3 batsmen + 1 bowler for Super Over.
  • If Super Over also ties → Boundary count decides winner.

8. Player Conduct

  • Verbal abuse, arguments, or fighting = **Immediate disqualification**.
  • Respect umpires. Their decision is **final and non-negotiable**.
  • Team must maintain discipline on and off the field.

9. Safety Rules

  • Helmets recommended for batsmen and close fielders.
  • No metal spikes allowed—only rubber shoes.
  • Injury time permitted for **maximum 5 minutes**.

10. Additional Tournament Rules

  • Captain must submit playing XI before toss.
  • Time-wasting by any side can lead to penalty runs.
  • Toss winners can choose **Bat** or **Ball**.
  • Teams failing to appear on time → **Walkover** (opponent wins).
